Minecraft Lore

Minecraft is one of the most popular survival games of all time. Do you know the lore behind the game itself? We will take a look into the lore and discuss the truth behind the drowned and the ocean monuments. Looking at minecraft from its in game "historical" background, we can dig deeper into the truth. Theories say that old civilzations existed and floursihed with people, before the natural disasters creating the world we see now. We can assume that the natural disasters are what placed monuments under water through out the game. This would also mean the people living in the monuments and around them would have died in the natural disasters. This is what we see today as the drowned and ocean monuments. The more you know :P
