Tips & Tricks

Alan Wake II

To open the gun case, use the numeric value for the first letter of
the alien books. use the decoder on the paper next to the books. This is
Saga's chapter 7.

Stardew Valley

Selling Tea Saplings in Stardew Valley's mid-late game is a huge
money source. Keep foraging so you can make multiple Tea Saplings at a time.
